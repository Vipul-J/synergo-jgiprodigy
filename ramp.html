Ramp Walk: Cyberverse

Walk into the art that ace the personality with lots of confidence elevate your style and walk the ramp like you own it and embrace the show.

Participant Skills:

  • Posing
  • Walking Techniques
  • Body Posture
  • Discipline
  • Flexibility
  • Teamwork
  • Professionalism and Ability to Take Direction

Rules & Regulations:

  • Theme for the ramp walk is Cyberverse.
  • Costumes must include the color 'chrome' based on the theme and every individual should have their own prop.
  • Team size: 8 participants + 3 for assistance. The participants should be the same from the existing 11 from other events.
  • Time limit: 6 minutes + 2 minutes extra for setup.
  • Costumes and props should be managed by participants according to the theme.
  • Decency in costume should be maintained, costumes portraying unwanted exposure are not allowed.
  • Props and costumes must not relate to any religious and political subjects, slogans, and are strictly restricted.
  • Background track is compulsory.
  • Judges decision will be final.
