What is Prodigy'24

Prodigy'24, comes under the umbrella of Parousia. It is national level fest organized by Jain College of BBA, BCA & Commerce, Department of BCA. Every year it is organized for 2 days with different events like Cyber-Security, Communication, Gaming, Coding, Best Manager, Designing and Cultural Activities. Teams from In and Around Belagavi and other state participate in the event actively.
